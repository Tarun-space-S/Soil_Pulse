Motive

Technology in agriculture, often referred to as AgTech, encompasses a range of innovations aimed at improving the efficiency and sustainability of farming practices. It includes the use of precision agriculture, drones, and satellite imagery for optimized crop management. Genetic engineering and biotechnology have led to the development of genetically modified crops with enhanced traits. Automation and robotics play a vital role in tasks like harvesting and weeding. IoT devices and sensors enable real-time data collection for informed decision-making. Additionally, AI and machine learning help analyze data, predict crop diseases, and manage resources more effectively, ultimately enhancing food production while reducing environmental impacts.
